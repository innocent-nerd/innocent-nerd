**Mitigating Sycophancy in LLMs — Research Paper** *(June 2026)*
Authored a research proposal addressing AI sycophancy — the tendency of LLMs to agree with false premises and flip stances under pressure. Proposed a three-phase methodology (adversarial dataset curation → SFT on candor demonstrations → modified RLHF) and an Indifferent Fact-Grounded Architecture (IFGA) that routes flagged prompts through a critique-forward decoding path.
